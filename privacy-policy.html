Privacy Policy

QueryBee Chatbot - M. L. Dahanukar College of Commerce

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to QueryBee, your AI-powered chatbot assistant for M. L. Dahanukar College of Commerce. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with our chatbot service.

By using QueryBee, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Chat Conversations

  • Messages: Your questions, queries, and interactions with the chatbot
  • Responses: The answers and information provided by the chatbot
  • Session Data: Session identifiers to maintain conversation context
  • Timestamps: When messages are sent and received

2.2. Technical Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the chatbot (clicks, session duration)
  • Performance Data: Response times and error logs for service improvement

2.3. Optional Information

  • Voice Data: If you use voice features, audio recordings may be processed
  • Feedback: Thumbs up/down feedback on chatbot responses
  • Preferences: Theme settings, font choices, and customization options

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1. Service Provision

  • To provide accurate and relevant responses to your questions
  • To maintain conversation context and improve user experience
  • To personalize interactions based on your preferences

3.2. Service Improvement

  • To analyze chat patterns and improve response accuracy
  • To identify and fix technical issues
  • To develop new features and capabilities

3.3. Analytics and Research

  • To understand user behavior and preferences
  • To generate anonymized usage statistics
  • To conduct research for educational service improvement

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1. Storage Duration

  • Chat History: Stored locally in your browser for up to 200 messages
  • Session Data: Temporary storage during active sessions
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and stored for service improvement

4.2. Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS protocols
  • Secure storage of conversation logs
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems

Important: Your chat conversations are primarily stored locally in your browser. You can clear your chat history at any time using the "Clear" button in the chatbot interface.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1. We Do Not Sell Your Information

QueryBee does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties.

5.2. Limited Sharing

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted service providers for technical operations
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or court order
  • College Administration: Anonymized usage statistics may be shared with college authorities for service improvement

5.3. AI Processing

Your questions are processed by Google Dialogflow AI services to generate responses. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information about their data handling practices.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1. Access and Control

  • View Chat History: You can view your conversation history at any time
  • Clear History: Use the "Clear" button to delete all stored conversations
  • Export Data: You can copy your chat conversations for personal records
  • Opt-out: You can stop using the service at any time

6.2. Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data that we hold about your interactions with QueryBee.

6.3. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data by clearing your browser data or contacting us directly.

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1. Local Storage

  • Chat History: Stored in browser's local storage for convenience
  • Preferences: Theme settings and customization options
  • Session Data: Temporary session identifiers

7.2. Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand how QueryBee is used, but this data is anonymized and aggregated.

8. Children's Privacy

QueryBee is designed for use by college students and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
